Why I like baking?

Once upon a time, I was a person who only liked to eat but have no interest in cooking. When studying in France, I couldn't adapt to the local diet and tried to cook for myself. Luckily, I found that I have a talent for baking and can easily make delicious meals.

After that, I tend to try some new desserts whenever I have time. Especially during the 2020 epidemic, I totally immersed myself in the kitchen and have mad many delicious desserts.
